About The Website

Art and culture is not explained enough and there isn't much representation to it especially in my culture which is in India. Therefore, this website is to inform people about how Art is a main part in culture and the meanings of different paintings. Through this website, it will explore the different painting that represent Indian culture and the meanings behind each of the paintings. It will talk about the history of art in India, as well as the summary of all the information. The reason as to why I have developed this website, is because of me living in a multicultural country I find it hard to explore other people's culture's and where they are from. Hence, creating this website would help people understand more about Hindu culture through art rather than a simple video or an essay
